FAQs

Clear answers on validation, methodology, and how we reduce your risk.

How long does it take?

A standard smoke test runs for 2 to 3 weeks; a low-code MVP takes 4 to 8. Our use of AI and no-code tools bypasses months of traditional development.

How do we source users for validation?

We handle the precise recruitment of users that match your target demographic and financial profile.

What if it fails?

That is validation. Discovering a flaw early costs a fraction of a late-stage failure. The data tells you whether to pivot, adjust, or kill the idea. That’s an informed decision.

What is the cost?

It varies based on your specific needs and scope. What we do know is that launching a financial product without validation guarantees a significantly higher cost.

How does this integrate with my existing workflow?

Think of us as your rapid-validation arm. We integrate technology that helps build a high-fidelity simulation of your product, seamlessly accompanying your team.
